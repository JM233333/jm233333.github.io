Given two words word1 and word2 , find the minimum number of operations required to convert word1 to word2 .

You have the following 333 operations permitted on a word:

  • Insert a character

  • Delete a character

  • Replace a character

题意

给定两个字符串 word1 和 word2 ，求将字符串 word1 转换为字符串 word2 所需的最少操作次数。操作有如下三种：

  1. 删除一个字符；

  2. 插入一个字符；

  3. 将一个字符改为另一个字符。

题解
