Given strings s1 , s2 , and s3 , find whether s3 is formed by an interleaving of s1 and s2 .

An interleaving of two strings s and t is a configuration where they are divided into non-empty substrings such that:

  • s = s1 + s2 + ... + sn

  • t = t1 + t2 + ... + tm

  • |n - m| <= 1

  • The interleaving is s1 + t1 + s2 + t2 + s3 + t3 + ... or t1 + s1 + t2 + s2 + t3 + s3 + ...

Note: a + b is the concatenation of strings a and b .

题意

给定三个字符串 s1 , s2 和 s3 ，判断 s3 是否可由 s1 和 s2 按照题目所述的规则转化得到。

题解

为便于描述，设字符串的下标从 111 起，字符串 s1 和 s2 的长度分别为 nnn 和 mmm 。

考虑动态规划，设 dp[i][j]dp[i][j]dp[i][j] 表示 s1[1, i]s_1[1,\ i]s1​[1, i] 和 s2[1, j]s_2[1,\ j]s2​[1, j] 是否能转化为 s3[1, i+j]s_3[1,\ i + j]s3​[1, i+j] 。

状态转移方程：

{dp[0][0]=1dp[i][0]=dp[i−1][0] & (s1[i]==s3[i])dp[0][j]=dp[0][j−1] & (s2[j]==s3[j])dp[i][j]=(dp[i−1][j] & (s1[i]==s3[i+j]))∣(dp[i][j−1] & (s2[j]==s3[i+j]))\begin{cases} dp[0][0] &= 1\\ dp[i][0] &= dp[i - 1][0]\ \&\ (s_1[i] == s_3[i])\\ dp[0][j] &= dp[0][j - 1]\ \&\ (s_2[j] == s_3[j])\\ dp[i][j] &= (dp[i - 1][j]\ \&\ (s_1[i] == s_3[i + j])) \mid (dp[i][j - 1]\ \& \ (s_2[j] == s_3[i + j])) \end{cases}⎩⎪⎪⎪⎨⎪⎪⎪⎧​dp[0][0]dp[i][0]dp[0][j]dp[i][j]​=1=dp[i−1][0] & (s1​[i]==s3​[i])=dp[0][j−1] & (s2​[j]==s3​[j])=(dp[i−1][j] & (s1​[i]==s3​[i+j]))∣(dp[i][j−1] & (s2​[j]==s3​[i+j]))​

答案即为 dp[n][m]dp[n][m]dp[n][m] 。时间复杂度为 O(nm)O(nm)O(nm) 。
